Ian Givin

Director

BA (Hons), Dip Arch, RIBA, ARB
UK

Ian is the founding Director of ARCHITECTS IN PRACTICE, having established the practice following extensive experience working for prominent London-based, award winning practices such as CGL and Stirling Prize winner, dRMM.

Having completed his under-graduate studies at the Manchester School of Architecture Ian went on to complete his post-graduate studies at the University of East London, where his work was awarded by the RIBA, before gaining professional qualification from the RIBA North West.

Having completed his under-graduate studies at the Manchester School of Architecture Ian went on to complete his post-graduate studies at the University of East London, where his work was awarded by the RIBA, before gaining professional qualification from the RIBA North West.

Throughout his professional career Ian has amassed over 15 years’ experience in the design and delivery of major works concentrated around ambitious residential, education, mixed-use and regeneration schemes across the UK. Ian’s strengths lie in his creative eye – which transcends into the buildings we design, his self-drive, his meticulous attention to detail and the management of complex production information. As the Founding Director of ARCHITECTS IN PRACTICE, Ian manages the strategic development and oversees the design, production and delivery of all projects.

Ian is a Northerner by birth, a Londoner by choice, an Architect by trade, a football and cycling enthusiast by default, and a record collector by instinct.
