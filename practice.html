Process

Analogue

Making physical models is a fundamental part of our design process.

We use physical models from the outset to convey qualities of the project that other methods cannot. We also find them to be an effective tool to communicate ideas with clients, planning authorities and project teams.

The process alone often brings out new design ideas for discussion, and we encourage all projects to be developed in this way, from simple concepts to presentation models, we find this approach encourages collaboration throughout, something we believe benefits the project as a whole.

Immersive Design

We integrate virtual and augmented reality into our daily BIM workflow.

With the use of cutting-edge virtual tools, we are able to delve into fully immersive environments to explore every area and aspect of a project. We find this to be an immensely powerful tool, one that can provide deeper understanding of the project and can offer greater efficiency with decision making.

Using real-time visualisation, we are able to gain consensus and move forward quickly with important design decisions.
